Typically found in the shallow waters of Mexico's Gulf of California, the shy and elusive Vaquita is the only species of porpoise that lives in warm waters. As a nod to the warm, coastal waters the elusive creature calls home, Vaquita blends an aquatic sea salt accord with fresh waterlily. Vibrant redcurrant berries and juicy pear give the fragrance a spirited personality rounded out with a touch of cedarwood. The result? A fragrance as enigmatic and playful as its namesake.

TOP NOTES: Water Lily, Sea Salt
HEART NOTES: Pear, Red Currant
BASE NOTES: Cedar
